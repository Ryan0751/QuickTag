The Genres Preferences Tab

The genres preferences tab allows you to edit the genre list:

Click +/- to add/remove genre entries. Double click on the genre name to modify the entry.

The "import" button allows to you import your existing genre's from iTunes:

NOTE: Importing from iTunes may take a few moments to complete.

Click on the "Name" heading to sort the list.
